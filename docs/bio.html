About the Astrologer

Meet the Astrologer

Sarvesh Dwivedi is a highly respected astrologer with over 30 years of experience in the field of Vedic astrology. A Ph.D. graduate in Astrology from the esteemed University of Allahabad in India, Sarvesh has dedicated his life to helping others gain insight, clarity, and direction through the ancient wisdom of the stars.

Throughout his career, he served at Pundit Junction, a renowned astrology consultancy, where he guided over 3,000 clients across the United States and Canada. His expertise spans multiple disciplines including birth chart readings, vaastu shastra, numerology, and gemstone recommendations, making him a sought-after consultant for those seeking both traditional and practical spiritual guidance.

With a deep understanding of cosmic patterns and their real-life applications, Sarvesh Dwivedi is known for his compassionate approach, accuracy, and ability to simplify complex astrological insights into meaningful life advice.
